Challenge

The purpose of Lab 12 is to build on our understanding of what we learned about CSS previously through styling our Lab 11 webpage using more rules of CSS that we learned. We were to add "Challenge," "Problems," and "Results" sections along with the corresponding information about our experiences with the lab. We were then to style the various texts using different methods of block-level styling such as text and image alignment. We were to utilize various elements utilized to either center our image/sample texts as vertically, horizontally, or floated to a certain side. As a bonus, we were to add a large image to a div and make the div smaller than the image, but make it so that the image is centered vertically and horizontally so you see only the middle part of the image. After doing so we are to test our files locally to see if everything works the way it should and then upload them to the server.
