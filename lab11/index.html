Challenge

The purpose of Lab 11 is to build on our understanding of what we learned about CSS previously through styling our Lab 11 webpage using more rules that we learned. We were to add "Challenge," "Problems," and "Results" sections along with the corresponding information about our experiences with the lab. We were then to style the various texts using different methods of block-styling (more specifically inline-block styling) and style our navigation links so that they stick to the top of the page when one scrolls down. As a bonus, we were to go back to our homepage and add a sticky menu or a sidebar to link to our labs and use relative, fixed, or absolute elements. After doing so we are to test our files locally to see if everything works the way it should and then upload them to the server.

Problems
