Lab 10: Styling Text Elements

Challenge

The purpose of Lab 10 is to build on our understanding of what we learned about CSS previously through styling our Lab 10 webpage using more CSS rules that we learned. We were to add "Challenge," "Problems," and "Results" sections along with the corresponding information about our experiences with lab. We were then to style the various texts using new fonts, colors, text-transform, and letter-spacing. As a bonus, we were to choose a Google font for our headings and also go back and style our homepage with our new knowledge about using fonts in CSS. After doing so we are to test our files locally to see if everything works the way it should and then upload them to the server.

Problems
