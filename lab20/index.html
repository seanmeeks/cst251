Lab 20: Team Project Sketches

Challenge

The purpose of Lab 20 was to create 15 "quick, fast, rough sketches" of different facets of our idea to develop a more detailed prototype of our client website. We were to complete this assignment individually. After creating the 15 sketches, we were to take pictures of/scan and upload them to our Lab 20 webpage. After doing so we are to test our files locally to see if everything works the way it should and then upload them to the server.

Problems

When completing Lab 20, the only issue was getting the menu icon to appear in the navbar when the navbar collapsed due to the page being shrunk. For this lab, I continued to use Bootstrap, however, this time I changed the navbar color. In order to do that, I had to set it as "navbar-custom" instead of the default options. I have no idea what happened to the menu icon when I did that. The menu icon is there, but it's somehow "invisible" and I have no idea how to make it visible again.
